Why this works with your Leaf Trail:

Pros

  • Grounding: The "Bento" cards provide solid points for the eye to rest, making the falling leaves feel like a magical overlay rather than background noise.
  • Glass Effect: The blurred cards (backdrop-filter) allow the leaves to pass "behind" the UI occasionally, creating amazing depth.
  • Clarity: We use clean, minimal backgrounds (deep brown/black for Sakura, stone for Momiji) so the colorful leaves really pop.

Cons

  • Requires careful z-index management to ensure the leaves don't get "stuck" under the bento boxes unless that's intentional.
